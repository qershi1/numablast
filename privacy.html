Privacy Policy

NUMA BLAST · Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information NUMA BLAST ("we", "the app") collects and how it is used. By playing NUMA BLAST you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. If you create an account or sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your display name and email address to identify your account.
  • Game progress. Your level, score, gold, gems, titles and settings. These are stored on your device and, for signed-in players, in our cloud database (Google Firebase).
  • Leaderboard data. If you are signed in, your display name, chosen avatar and best scores are stored so you can appear on the global leaderboard.

We do not collect your contacts, photos, location, or any sensitive personal data. Guest players are not required to provide any personal information.

2. How we use it

  • To save and sync your game progress across sessions and devices.
  • To display global and per-mode leaderboards.
  • To keep the game secure and functioning.

3. Third-party services

We use Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) to handle sign-in and store scores. Firebase processes data under Google's privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy. Sign-in with Google and Apple are governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Data retention & deletion

Your account data is kept while your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at the address below; we will delete it within 30 days.

5. Children's privacy

NUMA BLAST is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond the optional account email described above.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
